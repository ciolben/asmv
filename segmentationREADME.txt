To run the segmentation VIEWER from "Hierarchical Graph-Based Video Segmentation", follow these steps :
(Note : all executables and libs for Windows are present, libs for msvc2010)
1. Compute segmentations with http://neumann.cc.gt.atl.ga.us/segmentation/
2. Download segementation data
3. Call \asmv\segmentation_sample\build\Release\segmentation_sample.exe filename.pb
